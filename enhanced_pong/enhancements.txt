I have updated my game of pong to change the ball color when the balls hits any of the walls or the paddle.
If the paddle hits the paddle, it turns back to white.
If the paddle hits the top wall, it turns light yellow.
If the paddle hits the right wall, it turns light green.
If the paddle hits the bottom wall, it turns pink.

I used the variable mBall Color to pass RGBA values within the code to the ball color.
